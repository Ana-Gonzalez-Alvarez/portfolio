Ana is a second-year Computer Science student in the Honors College at Miami Dade College, Padron Campus. Her academic interests include cybersecurity, robotics, artificial intelligence, and the development of secure, socially responsible technologies. She conducts research in cybersecurity and post-quantum systems under the mentorship of Dr. Selcuk Uluagac at Florida International University and leads a sustainability-focused robotics project that connects autonomous systems with environmental education.
